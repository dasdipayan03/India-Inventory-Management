# India Inventory Management - Features and User Benefits

| Module                           | Features                                                                          | User Benefit                                                                           |
| -------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Purchase Entry / Add Stock       | Automatically adds/updates stock through supplier bill entry                      | Inventory updates directly from purchase bills                                         |
| Supplier Ledger                  | View supplier-wise purchase bills, paid/due amounts, and outstanding balances     | Easily track how much payment is pending for each supplier                             |
| Sale Entry / Invoice             | Create customer invoices, select items, calculate GST/total, and generate PDFs    | Faster billing, professional invoices, and easy PDF sharing with customers             |
| Stock View / Report              | View item-wise available quantity, buying price, selling price, and sold quantity | Easily understand current stock levels and identify low-stock items                    |
| Low Stock Alert                  | Shows alerts/reports when stock is running low                                    | Helps reorder products before stock runs out                                           |
| Sales View / Report              | View date-wise sales, invoice history, and totals                                 | Makes it easier to analyze business sales performance                                  |
| GST Report                       | Generates GST-wise tax reports                                                    | Keeps invoice-wise tax data ready for GST filing and checking                          |
| Customer Due                     | Track customer-wise due amounts, payment history, and ledger PDFs                 | Makes customer collection tracking easier and prevents missing outstanding dues        |
| Expense Tracking                 | Save and manage business expenses with reports                                    | Helps understand real profit, not just sales figures                                   |
| Dashboard Overview               | Displays stock value, low stock alerts, customer dues, and summary cards          | Gives owners a quick overview of overall business health                               |
| Staff Access Control             | Assign page-specific permissions to staff members                                 | Staff can access only assigned sections, keeping sensitive data secure                 |
| Role-based Login                 | Separate login system for owners and staff                                        | Keeps business control in the owner's hands                                            |
| Search Dropdowns                 | Autocomplete/search for items, suppliers, and customers                           | Faster data entry with fewer spelling mistakes and duplicate entries                   |
| PDF Downloads                    | Download invoices, reports, and customer ledger PDFs                              | Makes record keeping and sharing easy                                                  |
| Mobile Friendly UI               | Works smoothly on phones, browsers, and Android app wrappers                      | Enables billing and stock management directly from mobile devices                      |
| Refresh Button                   | Refreshes the current page/section without changing location                      | Quickly reloads the latest data without navigation hassle                              |
| Play Store Android App           | Available as an Android app wrapper on the Play Store                             | Users can access the app directly from an app icon instead of typing URLs in a browser |
| Secure Session & Access Handling | Blocks unauthorized actions and syncs staff permissions                           | Reduces permission-related issues and improves account security                        |
